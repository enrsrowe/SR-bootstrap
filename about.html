The Club

We are all volunteers! We are all involved in the Oval Ski Club because of our love of the sport.

The ski club team

The club has an elected committee and several qualified instructors. You can see our comittee and instructors below. However we also require additional helpers to make the club a success. If you are interested in helping out with the club on either a Saturday morning or Sunday evening then please contact us.

Where are we?

The Oval Sports Centre, Bebington, Wirral (Opposite Bebington Train Station). See the contact page for more details.
