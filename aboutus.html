About Us

We are all volunteers! We are all involved in the Oval Ski Club because of our love of the sport. As we are a members only club everyone must join prior to skiing. This only takes a few minutes and can be done on your first visit. All money raised through membership is used for equipment, instructor training etc so everyone benefits. You will also receive discounts from various local sports shops.

The club has an elected committee and several qualified instructors. You can see our comittee and instructors below. However we also require additional helpers to make the club a success. If you are interested in helping out with the club on either a Saturday morning or Sunday evening then please contact us.

We are located at the Oval Sports Centre, Bebington, Wirral (Opposite Bebington Train Station). See the contact page for more details.
